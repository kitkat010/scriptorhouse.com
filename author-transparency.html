Scriptor House Transparency Policy

Professional Clarity

Our Statement

Transparency Is Not An Afterthought.

It is part of how we work with authors from the beginning.

Scriptor House believes authors deserve clear expectations before entering a publishing, development, or creative services relationship. Our role is to provide professional support, structured guidance, and honest communication about what a service can and cannot provide.

Scope of Service

What We Provide. What We Do Not Promise.

Scriptor House provides professional publishing, development, and creative support. We do not present services as guaranteed outcomes.

Included

What We Help With

  • Editorial guidance
  • Publishing preparation
  • Book formatting support
  • Cover design coordination
  • Author positioning
  • Creative development consultation
Not Guaranteed

What We Do Not Guarantee

  • Bestseller status
  • Book sales or royalties
  • Reviews or media coverage
  • Film or television production
  • Producer or studio interest
  • Financial return or profit
Author Rights

Your Story. Your Ownership.

Working with Scriptor House does not transfer ownership of your work. We believe authors should clearly understand their rights before beginning any publishing or development journey.

Brand Clarity

Scriptor House And Scriptor House Films

These two brands may work with stories, authors, and creative properties, but they serve different purposes. Knowing the difference helps authors choose the correct pathway from the beginning.

Scriptor House

Publishing, Development, and Author Services

Scriptor House focuses on professional publishing support, author development, book preparation, creative positioning, and selected book-to-film development or outreach preparation.

  • Partnership publishing
  • Editorial and formatting support
  • Author positioning
  • Book-to-film evaluation
  • Producer-facing preparation
Scriptor House Films

Film Production Pathway

Scriptor House Films is the separate pathway for authors or project owners who want to explore author-funded film production, production coordination, and story-to-screen development.

  • Author-funded film production pathway
  • Story-to-screen coordination
  • Film production support
  • Production planning
  • Marketing and visibility after completion

Transparency Note

Scriptor House does not automatically produce films from books. If your goal is to produce your own film, that pathway belongs with Scriptor House Films. If your goal is publishing, author development, book-to-film evaluation, or producer-facing preparation, Scriptor House may be the correct starting point.

Security Notice

Verify Before You Respond.

Please be aware that unauthorized individuals may falsely claim to represent Scriptor House or Scriptor House Films.

Official communication should come only from verified company email addresses or confirmed company communication channels.

Main Scriptor House info@scriptorhouse.com
Scriptor House Films info@scriptorhousefilms.com
If you receive a suspicious message, payment request, invoice, contract, or service offer, contact us directly for verification before responding.

Official Channels Only

Never send manuscripts, personal information, payments, or signed documents through unverified accounts.
