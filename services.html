Understanding Your Options

Not All Publishing Paths Are The Same

Every publishing path serves a different purpose. Understanding the differences helps authors choose the approach that best supports their goals.

Traditional

Traditional Publishing

  • Publisher controls acceptance
  • Competitive submission process
  • Long evaluation timeline
  • Publishing decisions are made by the publisher
Self

Self Publishing

  • Complete creative control
  • Author manages every stage
  • Requires learning multiple publishing disciplines
  • Independent decision making
