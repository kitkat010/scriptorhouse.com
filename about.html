Who We Are

A narrative development company for authors with serious creative goals.

Scriptor House LLC is a narrative development and intellectual property positioning company dedicated to helping authors transform original stories into professionally structured creative properties.

We work with authors, storytellers, entrepreneurs, leaders, and creators who want their books and stories to be developed with care, structure, and long-term value.

Our work may include publishing support, editorial guidance, book design coordination, market positioning, cinematic development, producer-facing preparation, and strategic presentation.

To help the author's work become clearer, stronger, and more professionally prepared for the path ahead.

Our Approach

A Structured Path From Manuscript to Market

Scriptor House helps simplify the creative process by giving authors a clearer path from review to presentation.

01

Review

We review the manuscript, concept, or project information to understand its current stage, strengths, challenges, and professional needs.

02

Strategy

We help identify the most appropriate path based on the material, the author’s goals, and the project’s readiness.

03

Development

We assist with the preparation needed to strengthen the work, including editorial planning, publishing support, cinematic packaging, or market positioning.

04

Presentation

We help prepare the project for its intended audience, whether readers, retailers, reviewers, media contacts, producers, or industry professionals.

05

Follow-Through

We communicate with the author throughout the process so expectations, deliverables, and next steps remain clear.
